Cutout Kitchen .

Cutout Kitchen Mobile App    Registry & Survey

Cutout Kitchen is a web and mobile application designed for food lovers to experience new cultures. It gives users access to home-cooked meals by locating chefs in their area. Chefs have a chance to earn income by opening their homes to users. Donation mechanisms are embedded in the website as features to bring this experience to people and also create opportunities for low-income households to break out of the poverty cycle.
