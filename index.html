FAQ

What is Kitchen Salt?

Kitchen Salt App, is a web and mobile application that provides a platform for chefs to share their recipes, build their online portfolio and offer their culinary talents to serve the community. We provide opportunities for users to earn income for serving authentic cultural dishes and deliever them via Uber or other means.

Is Kitchen Salt Free?

Yes! You may download the app on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store upon release. Subscritions plans may vary.

How do I use Kitchen Salt Features?

To use the features, here are 7 simple steps:

1. Sign in.
2. Provide your info and build your profile.
3. Upload photos, video of your best dishes and talent.
4. Discover new followers and build your customer base.
5. Go to online mode and gear up for order requests.
6. a. Prepare your table for your walk-in orders.
6. b. Connect with Uber Business and Hail an uber to deliever the order the the customer.

7. Get paid for every transaction.

Which version of iOS do your apps support?

Kitchen Salt App,is supported on all versions of iOS and Android.

Do I keep all my profit?

As mentioned in a our Terms of Service, we will deduct 10% of every transaction as mark-up for our services.

I have technical problem, who do I email?

For all feedback and troubleshooting, message us on our Facebook page.
